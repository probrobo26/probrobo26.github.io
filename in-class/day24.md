## Deep Dive Presentations
We will be splitting class into two 45 minute portions: a public-facing poster session and a round of internal project pitching. 

### Public Poster Session
Members of the Olin community will be invited to participate from 1:15PM to 2:00PM. Please set up your poster/demos somewhere in the room in the first 15 minutes of class.

Members of the public will engage with your posters throughout the 45 minutes. They will have the ability [to fill in a form](https://forms.gle/hffH8cqb8D72kAwq6) providing brief feedback on the posters they engaged with. You will get access to this feedback at the end of the day; this feedback will be lightly incorporated into your presentation score.

### In-Class Shareout
For the remainder of class, we will do a brief round of project pitches -- every project will have ~5 minutes to provide the whole class with an overview of their project, key learning objectives, and things that they discovered. This is meant to be an informal and fun way to see what everyone had been working on for their deep dives! As we go around, [brief feedback can be submitted with this form](https://forms.gle/wj6HCPZBMstUzHU17) which will be lightly incorporated into your presentation score.
